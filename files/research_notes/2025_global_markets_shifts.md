# 2025 Global Market Shifts and Developments Research

## Emerging Markets Performance

Asian markets showed mixed performance, with India maintaining strong growth momentum while China faced continued property sector challenges. The MSCI Emerging Markets index gained 8% despite ongoing geopolitical tensions and trade uncertainties.

Latin American markets benefited from commodity price stability, with Brazil's Bovespa reaching record levels on agriculture exports and energy sector strength. Mexico's market gained 12% despite political volatility around elections.

## Currency Market Developments

The US dollar index (DXY) declined 5-7% in 2025 as rate differentials narrowed with other major economies. The euro strengthened to 1.15 against the dollar as ECB rate cuts lagged Fed easing.

Japanese yen showed significant volatility, initially weakening to 162 per dollar before strengthening to 145 as the BOJ raised rates. This carry trade unwinding created ripple effects across global markets.

## Trade and Supply Chain Evolution

Near-shoring and friend-shoring accelerated in 2025, with US companies reducing China exposure by 15-20%. Mexico and Vietnam emerged as primary beneficiaries of supply chain diversification.

Commodity markets stabilized with oil averaging $75-80 per barrel. Critical minerals for energy transition saw increased investment, with lithium and copper prices recovering from 2024 lows.
