# AI in Customer Service 2025

## Adoption and Deployment

80% of companies are either using or planning to adopt AI-powered chatbots for customer service by 2025, with 80% of customer service organizations using generative AI to enhance agent productivity. AI is projected to handle 95% of all customer interactions by 2025, encompassing both voice and text. According to Zendesk CEO Tom Eggemeier, advancement is moving toward 100% AI involvement in customer interactions. Future growth projections show 40% of enterprise applications will feature task-specific AI agents by 2026 (up from <5% in 2025), and 25% of organizations will use chatbots as primary service channels by 2027.

## Market Growth and ROI

The chatbot and AI customer service market experiences rapid expansion with the AI customer service market projected to reach $47.82 billion by 2030 at a 25.8% CAGR (2024-2030). The chatbot segment alone is expected to grow by $1.43 billion in 2025. Customer preference strongly favors AI: 51% of consumers prefer interacting with bots over humans for immediate service. Financial returns are compelling, with companies achieving average returns of $3.50 for every $1 invested in AI customer service, with leading organizations realizing up to 8x ROI.
