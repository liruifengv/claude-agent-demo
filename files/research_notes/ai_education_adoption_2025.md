# AI in Education 2025

## Adoption Rates Across Users

Education shows the highest AI adoption rate across all industries: 86% of education organizations use generative AI. Among teachers, 63% of K-12 educators have incorporated GenAI into teaching compared to 49% in higher education. Overall, 60% of educators use AI to improve and streamline daily responsibilities. Student adoption is even higher at 92% in 2025, up dramatically from 66% in 2024. Most strikingly, 88% of students use generative AI for assessments in 2025, up from 53% in 2024.

## Market Growth and Applications

The global AI education market reached $5.88 billion in 2024 and is projected to hit $32.27 billion by 2030, growing at 31.2% CAGR. Common teacher applications include research and content gathering (44%), creating lesson plans (38%), summarizing information (38%), and generating classroom materials (37%). This represents a fundamental shift in educational technology adoption, positioning AI as integral to modern teaching and learning.
