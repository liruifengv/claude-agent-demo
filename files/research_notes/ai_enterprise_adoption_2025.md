# AI Enterprise Adoption in 2025

## Overall Enterprise Implementation

87% of large enterprises have implemented AI solutions, with adoption expected to reach 91%+ by 2027. In 2025, generative AI adoption spans 71% of organizations using it in at least one business function, up from 65% in early 2024. Investment has surged dramatically: companies spent $37 billion on generative AI in 2025, compared to $11.5 billion in 2024â€”a 3.2x year-over-year increase. Average annual AI investment per organization reaches $6.5M.

AI agents mark a significant maturity milestone: 23% of organizations are scaling agentic AI systems, while 39% are experimenting. Overall, 79% have implemented AI agents at some level. Production maturity has doubled, with 31% of use cases reaching full production in 2025 versus 14% in 2024. Technology companies lead adoption at 94%, while healthcare and manufacturing rank among fastest-growing sectors. ROI is becoming evident: AI implementations save enterprises average $3.20 for every $1 invested.
