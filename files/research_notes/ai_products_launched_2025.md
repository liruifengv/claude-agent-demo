# AI Products and Features Launched in 2025

## Foundation Models and AI Platforms

Leading AI companies released powerful new models: Anthropic released Claude 4, Google unveiled Veo 3 (generating synchronized audio and video), and OpenAI launched GPT-5, described as their strongest generative model focused on flexible reasoning and multimodal capabilities. Anthropic also announced Claude Enterprise to compete with OpenAI's ChatGPT Enterprise, targeting enterprise customers with enhanced features.

## Enterprise AI Infrastructure

Google Cloud previewed six new AI agents for developers, data scientists, and data engineers to address operational and analytical needs. NVIDIA launched NIMs (modular, GPU-optimized inference microservices) and Enterprise Agent Runtime, providing standardized frameworks for deploying persistent, tool-using, multistep AI agents. ## Government and Regulated Industries

The US FDA launched Elsa, a generative AI platform operating in secure GovCloud environment, giving FDA employees access to internal documents while ensuring data remains on-premise. This represents significant adoption in highly regulated government sectors.
